About Me

I’m a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Zhejiang University, China, supervised by Prof. Wenbo Shen. I am also a member of the AAA CTF team (plz ddw) and my research interests include software and system security (macOS/Android/iOS). I love pure/instrumental and pop music.
